Contributing Factors Recorded by Toronto Police

The collision data (KSI) provided on the Toronto Police Service’s Open Data Portal includes a record of the factors that were determined by the police to have played a role in each of the collisions. This filter allows the collision data to be sorted by these contributing factors.

“Aggressive Driving” is one of the seven emphasis areas outlined in the City of Toronto’s Vision Zero Road Safety Plan.
